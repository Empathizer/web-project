ABOUT US

How did it all start? Zameen.com was the brainchild of two British-Pakistani entrepreneur brothers, Zeeshan Ali Khan and Imran Ali Khan. Their mission was to make Pakistani real estate accessible and convenient for everyone. Together, they proceeded to lead Zameen.com from a small start-up to the premier real estate entity of Pakistan. Zameen.com started its funding first round in 2012 which constituted of several angel investors and substantial investment from venture capital firms. A further two rounds of major investment were closed in late 2015 and early 2016, bringing in a total of $29 million.
